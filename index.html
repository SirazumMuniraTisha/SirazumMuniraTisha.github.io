About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at LSU and working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the BRBytes project. Currently, I am developing an automated grading system for the Introduction to Computational Thinking (ICT) course. I have been actively involved in various inter-disciplinary researches. I achieved my master's in the field of Computer Graphics and Image processing. My research interest includes Automated Grading Systems, Computer Science Education and Curriculum Development, Machine Learning, 3D Modelling, Computer Graphics, and Video Game Design.
